Our Mission

Bite Finder is a free, open-source web application designed to help users discover and explore restaurants. Our mission is to make dining exploration easy, engaging, and accessible for everyone. Users can browse, search, and view restaurant details, helping them find new and exciting dining experiences.

We aim to provide an intuitive and interactive platform for both food enthusiasts and casual diners. By keeping Bite Finder open-source, we encourage collaboration, innovation, and community-driven improvement within the world of food discovery.
