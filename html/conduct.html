At Bite Finder, we are committed to creating a welcoming, safe, and inclusive environment for everyone. All community members, contributors, and users are expected to follow this Code of Conduct.

1. Be Respectful

Respect all members of the community, regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

2. No Harassment

Harassment, discrimination, and offensive language or behavior will not be tolerated in any form.

3. Collaborate Constructively

Offer constructive feedback and help others learn. Avoid unhelpful criticism or personal attacks.

4. Respect Privacy

Do not share personal or confidential information about others without consent.

5. Follow Event Guidelines

When participating in events or discussions, follow any additional rules or guidelines provided by organizers.

By participating in the Bite Finder community, you agree to follow this Code of Conduct. Failure to comply may result in warnings or removal from community events and platforms.
